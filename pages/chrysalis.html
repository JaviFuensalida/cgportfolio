Chrysalis is a project about mental health, born from the need to express my anxiety. The focus of the entire series is the inner world as a dark place where to seek for solance and peace, with a strong connection to nature as a primitive condition of the being. This all process is compared to the metamorphosis of the larva from its first inmature stage to a more complete form, the imago. Like the nymph, we need to trasfigure into a chrysalis, accept ouselves and gain a new form of consciusness, that will allow us to turn into a more mature being, leaving behind the empty cocoon.
